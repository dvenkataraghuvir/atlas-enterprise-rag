ENTERPRISE SUPPORT SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
Document Owner: Customer Success
Last Updated: Q1 2024
Applies to: Enterprise and Enterprise+ subscription tiers

1. SUPPORT TIERS

Enterprise customers receive:
- 24/7 email and chat support
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM) for accounts >$50k ARR
- Access to the private enterprise support portal
- Quarterly business reviews

Enterprise+ customers additionally receive:
- Phone support during business hours (9am–6pm customer timezone)
- Escalation path to senior engineering on critical issues
- Custom SLA addendums available

2. RESPONSE TIME COMMITMENTS

Severity 1 (Production outage, data loss):
- First response: within 1 hour, 24/7
- Status update cadence: every 2 hours until resolved
- Target resolution: 4 hours

Severity 2 (Significant feature degradation):
- First response: within 4 hours, 24/7
- Status update cadence: every 8 hours
- Target resolution: 24 hours

Severity 3 (Minor bug, workaround available):
- First response: within 1 business day
- Target resolution: 5 business days

Severity 4 (Feature request, question):
- First response: within 2 business days
- No resolution SLA; handled through product roadmap process

3. UPTIME GUARANTEE

Enterprise SLA: 99.9% monthly uptime (excluding scheduled maintenance)
Enterprise+: 99.95% monthly uptime

Downtime is calculated as total minutes of unavailability in a calendar month divided by total minutes in the month.

Scheduled maintenance windows: Sundays 2am–6am UTC. Customers are notified 5 business days in advance via email and status page.

4. SLA CREDITS

If uptime falls below the committed threshold, customers are eligible for service credits:

99.5%–99.9%: 10% credit of monthly fee
99.0%–99.5%: 25% credit of monthly fee
Below 99.0%: 50% credit of monthly fee

Credits are applied to the next invoice. Credits do not carry over beyond 90 days and are not redeemable for cash.

To request a credit, submit a ticket tagged "SLA Credit Request" within 30 days of the affected period.

5. EXCLUSIONS

SLA commitments do not apply to:
- Scheduled maintenance windows
- Incidents caused by customer configuration or misuse
- Force majeure events
- Beta or preview features (clearly labeled as such)

6. DATA REGIONS

Enterprise customers may select from the following data residency regions:
- US East (default)
- US West
- EU West (Frankfurt)
- Asia Pacific (Singapore)

Region selection is configured at account creation. Region migrations require a support ticket and 30 days notice.
